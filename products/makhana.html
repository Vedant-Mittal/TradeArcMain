About Makhana

Makhana, also known as fox nuts, are popped seeds from the lotus plant. Naturally grown in Bihar's wetlands, these nutritious seeds are processed into crunchy, protein-rich snacks perfect for health-conscious consumers worldwide.

Our premium makhana is carefully harvested, processed under strict quality controls, and available in various forms to meet your specific market requirements.
